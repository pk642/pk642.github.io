Objectives

  • Provide understanding of AI concepts and tools.
  • Train educators in AI-driven teaching strategies.

  • Foster a community of AI-enabled educators.

Target Audience

Designed for teachers of degree colleges who wish to enhance their AI literacy and integrate it into education and research.
