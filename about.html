About MMTTP

The Malaviya Mission Teacher Training Programme (MMTTP) aims to transform higher education by embedding Indian values and ethos into teaching, research, publications, patents, and institutional development.

About Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence refers to systems capable of tasks requiring human-like intelligence such as decision-making, problem-solving, and learning. Educators can use AI to personalize learning and enhance student engagement.
